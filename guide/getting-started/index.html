The shape of things

Everything here is static and local. There is no backend, no account, and no network traffic beyond loading the page itself. Tools that remember anything use your browser's own storage and nothing leaves your device.

Moving around

Press / on any page to open the command palette and jump straight to a tool or guide. The coloured dots in the top bar switch the accent between green, amber and cyan — your choice is remembered on this device.

GETTING STARTED

A short orientation to what lives on this node and how the pieces fit.

The shape of things

Everything here is static and local. There is no backend, no account, and no network traffic beyond loading the page itself. Tools that remember anything use your browser's own storage and nothing leaves your device.

Tools

  • Fraktur Forge — transmute plain text into Unicode Fraktur for titles, tags, and other symbolic damage.
  • Notepad — a throwaway scratch buffer that auto-saves to this browser.
  • Countdown — set a duration and watch it drain; flares and sounds a tone at zero.

The short version

Everything happens in your browser. There is no backend to send data to, no analytics, no cookies for tracking, and no accounts. Once the page has loaded you can disconnect from the network and every tool still works.

What stays on your device

  • Notepad saves its contents to this browser's localStorage. It never leaves your machine and you can wipe it with the clear button.
  • Your accent-theme choice is remembered the same way, under a single localStorage key.
  • Every other tool — text transforms, ciphers, hashing, key generation — processes input in memory and keeps nothing.

Keys and hashes

Keymaker and Digest use the browser's built-in Web Crypto API. Generated passwords and computed hashes are produced locally and never transmitted.

Offline

A service worker caches the pages you visit, so the site is installable and keeps working without a connection. You can confirm this for yourself: load a tool, switch off your network, and reload.
